Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

Introduction

YUTorah Transcriber ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension and web application handle information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Our extension and service collect minimal information:

  • YUTorah URLs: When you click the "Transcribe Shiur" button, we receive the URL of the YUTorah page you're viewing to process the audio file.
  • Audio Files: We temporarily download MP3 files from YUTorah to generate transcription notes. These files are deleted immediately after processing.
  • Generated Notes: We cache the generated notes associated with each URL to provide faster responses for repeated requests.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect IP addresses or device information
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Download and process audio files from YUTorah
  • Generate AI-powered transcription notes using Google Gemini
  • Cache results to improve performance and reduce processing time

Data Storage and Retention

  • Temporary Files: MP3 files are stored temporarily during processing and deleted immediately after transcription is complete.
  • Cache: Generated notes are cached on our server to improve performance. Cache data may be cleared periodically.
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not permanently store audio files or personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Audio files are sent to Google's Gemini API for transcription. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing. View Google's Privacy Policy
  • YUTorah: We access publicly available audio files from YUTorah.org.

Permissions

The Chrome extension requests the following permissions:

  • Host Permissions (*.yutorah.org): Required to detect when you're on a YUTorah page and to capture the page URL.

We do not request or use any other permissions.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data during transmission and processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@yutorahnotes.com

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it
  • Request information about what data we have cached (contact us via email)
  • Request deletion of cached data associated with specific URLs

Consent

By using YUTorah Transcriber, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our processing of information as described herein.
